Skill

NY Bae PDP Builder

A Claude skill that walks you through building an NY Bae product display page on the Purplle app — start to finish. It conceptualises what goes on each PDP slide, writes the on-design copy in the NY Bae voice, drafts a simple image-generation prompt per slide, and reviews your finished designs. It does not generate images itself.

How to use

  1. Download the skill folder and add it to your Claude skills directory.
  2. Open Claude and mention "NY Bae PDP", "NYBae PDP", or any NY Bae product — the skill triggers automatically and shows its how-to-use guide.
  3. Step 1 — tell it the product you're making a PDP for, and two core USPs (one primary, one secondary).
  4. Step 2 — it proposes a 6–7 slide PDP structure for you to refine.
  5. Step 3 — design each slide one by one: layout + copy in the NY Bae voice.
  6. Step 4 — get a simple image-generation prompt for each slide.
  7. Step 5 — upload your finished designs one by one and get layout and copy feedback.

What you'll need

  • Claude access (Pro, Team, or Max)
  • The skill installed in your Claude skills directory
  • A separate image-generation tool (e.g. Higgsfield NBP) for actually generating the slide images — the skill writes the prompts; it does not generate images itself
