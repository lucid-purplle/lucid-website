AI FAQs

Straightforward answers to the questions we hear most. No jargon, no fluff.

What is AI?

+

AI (Artificial Intelligence) is software that learns patterns from huge amounts of data and uses those patterns to generate content, make decisions, or complete tasks. The tools we use day-to-day at Purplle — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — are large language models, trained on text from across the internet. Think of them as extremely capable writing and thinking assistants you can talk to in plain English.

Is it safe to share company details with AI?

+

Be careful about what you paste in. On free tiers, avoid sharing anything sensitive — passwords, customer PII, financial data, unreleased product formulations, internal strategy decks, or employee information. Free tools may use your inputs to train their models.

Paid plans (ChatGPT Team/Enterprise, Claude Pro/Team, Gemini Workspace) generally don't train on your data, but you should still use judgment for highly confidential material. If you're unsure about your plan's data policy or what's safe to share, check with Lucid before pasting.

Do I need tech knowledge to use AI?

+

No. Modern AI tools are built around plain conversation — you type what you want in English, and the AI responds. No code, no formulas, no technical setup. If you can write an email or a brief, you can use AI. Lucid's workflows are designed specifically for non-technical teams, with ready-made prompt templates so you don't have to figure things out from scratch.

Which AI is the best?

+

There's no single "best" — each tool has its own personality and strengths.

ChatGPT (OpenAI) — The all-rounder. Great for brainstorming, data analysis with file uploads, and quick everyday tasks. Has the widest plugin and integration ecosystem.

Claude (Anthropic) — Best for long-form writing, nuanced reasoning, careful editing, and tasks where tone and detail matter. Handles very long documents well and tends to be more thoughtful in its responses.

Gemini (Google) — Best when you need real-time info from Google Search, or when you're working inside the Google Workspace ecosystem (Docs, Sheets, Gmail). Gemini Nano Banana Pro is currently the best AI model for image generation — sharper, more accurate, and more controllable than anything else out there.
