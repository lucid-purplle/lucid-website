Prompt Library

PDP Video Prompts

A growing library of ready-to-use image and video prompts for producing PDP videos across brands. Each product has its own sheet with shot-by-shot prompts — hero shot, concern, texture, application, before/after, beauty shot, closing — written for Nano Banana Pro and Seedance 2.0.

How to use

  1. Open the sheet and find the product you're working on (one sheet per product).
  2. Each row is one shot. Read the Scene column for what the shot is, then copy the Image Prompt into Nano Banana Pro to generate the frame.
  3. Once the image is approved, copy the Video Prompt into Seedance 2.0 or Kling to animate it.
  4. Repeat for every shot in the product's sequence, then edit the frames together into the final PDP video.

What you'll need

  • Access to the Google Drive folder (under the Glamrs Google login)
  • Higgsfield access for Nano Banana Pro (image generation)
  • Seedance 2.0 or Kling access for video animation
  • The product's reference images (white-background product shot, model shot) — both are needed for consistency across frames
