AI Banner Creator Workflow

Create AI-designed platform banners for any Purplle product, end to end: from product research and conversion strategy through copy, social proof, model styling, and the final Nano Banana Pro banner generation.

When to use this: For creating AI-designed platform banners for any Purplle product from scratch.

What this is not: This isn’t for PDP or product photography image creation.

How to use: Work through the 9 steps below in order, in one continuous GPT chat. Each step builds on the last. Steps 1–7 are conversational with the GPT — it produces outputs you review and iterate on. Step 9 hands off to Nano Banana Pro for image generation. Higgsfield uses the shared team Glamrs login.

Step 01 Starter Prompt Paste the starter prompt into a new GPT chat. Step 02 Input Collection Share whatever product info you have — PDP link, reviews, BM notes. Step 03 Product Info Card The GPT produces a structured Product Info Card from your inputs. Step 04 Conversion Angle Lock the strongest conversion angle for the banner. Step 05 Copy Routes Get 3 headline + subline copy options. Optionally upload the Copy Framework for more. Step 06 Social Proof Pick one social proof cue that supports — doesn’t repeat — the headline. Step 07 Model Styling Lock the model profile — age, vibe, clothing, hair, accessories. Step 08 Visual Description Lock a plain-English description of how the banner should look. Step 09 NBP Prompting + Iteration Hand off to Nano Banana Pro, generate the banner, iterate.

What you'll need

  • An active LLM subscription (the workflow is written for GPT)
  • Higgsfield access for Nano Banana Pro (Step 9)
  • Your product’s Purplle PDP and reviews, or its internal research documents
